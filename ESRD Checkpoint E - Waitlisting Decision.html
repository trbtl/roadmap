KIDNEY TRANSPLANT ROAD MAP
CHECKPOINT E:
Waitlisting Decision
NN
If you are WAITLISTED:
Now that you are active on the kidney transplant waitlist, you should stay healthy and
be ready for surgery when a kidney becomes available.
See Checkpoint G for more information on staying active on the waitlist.
NN
If you are NOT WAITLISTED:
Not being approved doesnt always mean a No, but maybe a not just yet. Work with
your care team to help address any issues that can turn a no to a yes. If you have
questions about your status on the waitlist, ask the team at the transplant center.
See Checkpoint F for more information if youre not approved.
As you nish your transplant evaluation requirements, the transplant team will review
your case and decide if you are a transplant candidate.
