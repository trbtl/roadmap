Meet with your care team and transplant center to make a plan to address any denial

NN
Dialysis and Medication Compliance
· Going to dialysis regularly tells the transplant center you will take good care of the
new kidney and can improve recovery after surgery.
· If you miss multiple treatments, you may be placed on probation. If you have any
questions, talk to your dialysis care team.
NN
Weight loss: Ask for a referral for a weight loss program and work with your dietician to
make healthy food choices.
NN
Substance use disorders (illicit drug use, marijuana, tobacco, alcohol): Ask your
dialysis social worker to provide a list of programs in your area to help you quit.
NN
Mental health: Schedule an appointment with a mental health provider to address any
issues.
NN
Loss of transplant support partner: Work with your care team to identify additional
people who can help.
NN
Missing phone calls with the transplant center: Plan to check in with your transplant
care team regularly.
NN
Additional required tests or goals: Complete on time and notify the transplant center
when done.
